About Us

The Restuarant

Nami offers a contemporary twist on traditional Japanese cuisine set in historic Atwells Avenue in the Federal Hill district of Providence, Rhode Island. Serving sushi, hibachi, steak, and kitchen entrees with modern New American influences, Nami is the new face of Japanese fine dining in Providence. We offer a full-service bar and lounge with an extensive wine list and specialty cocktails.

Here To Serve You

Our sushi chefs and kitchen cooks are trained in both new and traditional styles of Japanese cuisine. That means there is sure to be a favorite dish for all, no matter the palate. Make a reservation with your friends or simply just walk in alone to unwind or hang out with our friendly restaurant staff. We pride ourselves on being the best Japanese restaurant in our neighborhood.
